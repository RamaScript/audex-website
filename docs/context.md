Build a premium enterprise website for **Audex Logistic Labs Private Limited**.

## 3. Brand Personality and Tone

Audex tone must be:

- Intelligent, not complicated
- Authoritative, not arrogant
- Precise
- Financially sharp
- Tailored, never generic
- Direct and efficient
- Strategic
- Trustworthy
- Insightful
- Efficient

Writing style:
- Clear, sharp, structured
- No vague SaaS fluff
- No generic phrases like "we leverage advanced technology"
- Speak in business impact, rupees, margins, visibility, control, recovery, and auditability
- Focus on outcomes, not feature dumping

Good tone example:
> We uncover where logistics costs leak вЂ” and show you how to stop it.

Bad tone example:
> We leverage advanced analytical frameworks to optimise freight expenditure.
